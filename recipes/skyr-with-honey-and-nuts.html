Skyr with honey and nuts

Skyr with honey and nuts is a delightful and nutritious Icelandic-inspired dish.
Skyr, a thick and creamy dairy product similar to yogurt but with a milder flavor and higher protein content, serves as the perfect base.
The addition of honey adds a natural sweetness, balancing the slight tanginess of the skyr.
Topping it off with a variety of nuts, such as almonds, walnuts, or hazelnuts, introduces a satisfying crunch and enhances the dish with healthy fats and a rich, nutty flavor.
This combination creates a harmonious blend of textures and tastes, making it an ideal option for breakfast, a snack, or a light dessert.

Ingredients

  • Skyr (you can use greek yogurt in case you can't get Skyr)
  • Honey
  • Mixed nuts
  • Mixed Berries(optional).

Steps

  • Preparte the nuts
    • Take a cloth,and double in half twice
    • Put the nuts on the cloth so they are completly covered inside the cloth
    • Punch or smash the cloth a few times with the nuts inside
  • Serving
    • Divide the Skyr in the desired amount of bowls
    • Drizzle the Skyr with honey
    • Add the nuts on top
    • Add Berries
